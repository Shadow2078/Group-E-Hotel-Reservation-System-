FAQs

Some of the frequenlty asked questions by the users

How do I search for hotels by price?

In the selection box at the top left of the search page you can sort the listing of hotels by price when searching for available hotels on a specific date.

Can I arrive in hotel before check in ?

Hotels will let you check-in significantly earlier than the published check-in time if a room is available and has been cleaned otherwise you will have to wait for the staffs to be done housekeeping.

Do I pay a reservation fee?

No, you dont have to pay for reservation .

Where can I find the contact details of the hotel?

You can find the address on the hotel page once you click in the certain page.
